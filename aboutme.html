About Me

Jerry Chun-Wei Lin is the leader of IKE Lab. He received his Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Cheng Kung University, Tainan, Taiwan in 2010. He is currently a full Professor with the Department of Computer Science, Electrical Engineering and Mathematical Sciences, Western Norway University of Applied Sciences, Bergen, Norway. He has published more than 500+ research articles in refereed journals (380+ SCI/SSCI with 90+ IEEE/ACM journals) and international conferences, 16 edited books, as well as 33 patents (held and filed, 3 US patents). He is the Editor-in-Chief of the International Journal of Data Science and Pattern Recognition, the Associate Editor of several top-tier journals including IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TCYB, IEEE TDSC, INS, KAIS, ISA Transactions, HCIS, IJIMAI, HCIS, JIT, IEEE Access, PlosOne, JCSC, and AIHC. He has recognized as the Most Cited Chinese Researcher respectively in 2018, 2019, 2020 and 2021 by Elsevier/Scopus. He is ranked as 9th most productive research in Norway regarding his publication from 2017-2020, the 2nd most productive researcher in Norway in 2022 based on 2021 publications. He is the Fellow of IET (FIET), the ACM Distinguished Scientist, and the IEEE Senior Member.

Research Interests:Big data mining and analytics; Machine and deep learning; Optimization; Bio-informatics; IoT applications; Privacy preservation and security
